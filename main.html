About

Lida Yudina is a writer and a distinguished painter especially known for her paintings of domestic and suburban landscapeIn 1967 she graduated from The National Academy of Fine Art and Architecture of Ukraine (Master of Fine Arts in Oil Painting), where she was influenced by the works of the prominent impressionists.

Lidiya Yudina is focused on still-lives and landscapes, painted on location (an plein air).
